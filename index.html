05 / Why We Built This

An RT Built This.
For Every RT.

Every question on this platform was written or reviewed by someone who has actually managed a ventilator at 3am, interpreted the ABG, called the physician, and documented the intervention. Not an algorithm. Not a textbook author who hasn't touched a patient in 20 years. A working RT.

The existing TMC prep platforms treat respiratory therapy like a trivia game. We treat it like what it is: a clinical discipline with life-and-death stakes that deserves preparation built by people who understand those stakes firsthand.

And this is just the beginning. TMC Prep is the foundation of something larger — a commitment to the profession from end to end. From student to Senior RT. From the exam room to the ICU to the future of clinical intelligence.
