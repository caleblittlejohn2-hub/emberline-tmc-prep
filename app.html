NBRC 2027 Exam Matrix — RT Examination Replaces TMC
Critical
Effective January 2027, the TMC Examination is replaced by the Respiratory Therapy (RT) Examination. 185 items over 4 hours. Two cut scores remain. Updated weighting on clinical decision-making and AI-assisted workflows.
