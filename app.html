Live Clinical Updates
The latest AARC, NBRC, AHA, and CDC guideline changes — surfaced so your exam prep stays current.
NBRC 2027 Exam Matrix — RT Examination Replaces TMC
Critical
Effective January 2027, the Therapist Multiple-Choice (TMC) Examination is replaced by the Respiratory Therapy (RT) Examination. New format: 185 multiple-choice items (160 scored + 25 pretest) over 4 hours. Two cut scores remain for CRT and RRT credentials. Updated content weighting with increased emphasis on clinical decision-making, AI-assisted clinical workflows, and evidence-based practice. If you're testing before January 2027, study to the current TMC matrix.
NBRC · Effective January 2027
AARC Clinical Practice Guideline — HFNC ROX Index Threshold
Updated
ROX index (SpO2/FiO2 ÷ respiratory rate) threshold for HFNC failure prediction updated. Current evidence: ROX < 4.88 at 12 hours identifies high risk for HFNC failure with strong sensitivity and specificity. This is high-yield for the Therapy domain — expect clinical scenario questions on when to escalate from HFNC to NIV or invasive ventilation.
AARC · February 2026
AHA 2025 CPR & ECC Guidelines — Ventilation Updates
Review
Updated guidance on ventilation during CPR. Key RT-relevant changes: bag-mask ventilation technique refinements, tidal volume targets during cardiac arrest (avoid hyperventilation), ETT confirmation protocol emphasis, and PETCO2 targets post-ROSC (>10 mmHg associated with better outcomes, target 35-45 mmHg). Know the 30:2 ratio for single rescuer and asynchronous ventilation during advanced airway management.
AHA · November 2025
CDC/HICPAC VAP Prevention Bundle — Revised Guidance
Updated
Updated VAP prevention bundle reinforces subglottic secretion drainage for all patients ventilated >48 hours, daily SAT/SBT pairing protocol, CHG oral care q4-8h, and HOB elevation 30-45°. The Infection Control domain consistently tests VAP bundle components — know each element and the evidence behind it. New emphasis: care bundle compliance tracking and RT role in daily weaning assessment.
CDC/HICPAC · January 2026
NBRC Free Self-Assessment Examination — Use Strategically
Strategy
The NBRC offers two free TMC self-assessment examinations using identical software to the real exam. Strategic use: take one at the start of your prep for baseline domain scores, save the second for 2-3 weeks before your exam date. NBRC provides a detailed score breakdown by content area — use this data to direct your TMC Prep study focus to your weakest domains.
